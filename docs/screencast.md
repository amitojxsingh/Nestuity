# Nestuity Screencast

Take a quick tour of **Nestuity**, the ultimate parenting companion that helps families save time, stay organized, and manage their budget efficiently.

In this screencast, you'll see:

- **Personalized Dashboard:** Quickly access your baby's milestones, reminders, stock of diapers, and more.
- **Price Analysis:** Compare real-time prices across multiple retailers and view historical trends to make smarter purchasing decisions.
- **Usage Calculator:** Track how many diapers your baby uses, check your stock, and see how long it will last.
- **Intelligent Reminders:** Get notified of tasks, vaccinations, and other important activities on a daily, monthly, quarterly, or yearly basis.
- **Email Alerts:** Receive weekly emails to remind you of upcoming tasks and when your supplies are running low.
